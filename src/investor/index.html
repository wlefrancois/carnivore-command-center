Investor Overview

Carnivore Command Center is a metabolic intelligence and performance tracking platform focused on helping users turn daily signals into action.

Company snapshot

CCC is being built as a focused digital health and performance product designed to combine simple daily tracking, readiness scoring, actionable recommendations, and self-experiment support into one streamlined platform.

Problem

Many users have access to health data, but most tools do not clearly connect that data to simple daily decisions. People often want to know what is helping, what is hurting, and what to improve next.

Solution

CCC simplifies this process by helping users log core daily signals, score readiness, identify limiting factors, and generate forward-looking guidance around recovery, performance, and consistency.

Target market

Health-conscious users, biohackers, carnivore and keto communities, performance-minded adults, and users seeking simpler metabolic health visibility.

Business model

Subscription software with potential expansion into premium analytics, family profiles, reports, experiments, coaching tools, and future partnerships.

Product direction

Readiness scoring, intelligent recommendations, weekly reporting, self-experiment tracking, and future recovery and training guidance.

Current stage

CCC is in active product build and beta-stage development. Core platform components include user accounts, daily logging, dashboard analytics, readiness scoring, recommendations, weekly summary logic, and forecasting foundations.
