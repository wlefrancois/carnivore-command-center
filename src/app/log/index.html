How to rate your scores (quick guide)

Rule of thumb: rate based on how your body actually performed today, not how you “wish” it felt.

Use anchors: pick the closest description (2 / 5 / 8) and adjust slightly.

Consistency beats precision: if you rate the same way each day, CCC can find patterns even if you’re imperfect.

Examples
2: dragging, hard to focus, basic tasks feel heavy
5: functional, can do normal day but not sharp
8: strong drive, clear focus, easy to initiate tasks
Tip: if you needed caffeine just to feel normal, you’re probably ≤5.
