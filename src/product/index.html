What you get inside the platform

CCC is built to move from passive tracking to useful daily action.

Metabolic Readiness Score

A simple daily score that helps you quickly understand whether your system looks strong, moderate, or strained.

Today’s Recommendation

Guidance based on what is currently limiting your score most, such as hydration, sleep, inflammation, or digestion.

Recovery Priorities

A ranked view of the areas that deserve your attention if you want a better result tomorrow.

Daily guidance to help decide whether today looks better for strength work, moderate effort, light movement, or recovery.

Summaries that show average score, best day, most common limiter, and how your recent week is trending.

A structured way to test changes in hydration, sleep, nutrition, electrolytes, and other recovery variables over time.
