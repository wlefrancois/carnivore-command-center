What Carnivore Command Center helps you do

CCC is designed to turn daily tracking into decision-making.

Metabolic Readiness

See a simple daily readiness score that reflects how your body is doing based on the signals you log.

Recovery Priorities

Find out which areas need the most attention today, such as hydration, sleep, inflammation, or digestion.

Training Guidance

Use readiness to guide whether today is better for strength training, light cardio, recovery work, or avoiding unnecessary max effort.

Weekly Reports

Review how your week performed, identify your best day, and spot the limiting factors that keep showing up.

Forecasting

Get forward-looking guidance that helps you understand how improving one variable may influence tomorrow’s readiness.

Experiment Mode

Run structured experiments around nutrition, hydration, sleep, electrolytes, and recovery habits to discover what actually works for you.
