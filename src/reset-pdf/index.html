7-Day Carnivore Reset Guide

A simple plan to rebuild momentum, reduce cravings, simplify meals, and track how your body responds.

How To Use This Guide

Keep meals simple, track daily signals, and look for patterns.

1. Keep Meals Simple
Beef, eggs, bacon, chicken, fish, salt, water.
2. Track Signals
Energy, cravings, digestion, sleep, mood.
3. Win The Day
Consistency beats perfect.

Before You Start

  • Pick foods you can repeat for 7 days.
  • Choose a daily CCC check-in time.
  • Remove snacks, sweeteners, alcohol, and keto treats.
  • Keep water and salt consistent.

Days 1–3: Build The Base

Day 1 — Clean Start

Focus: Remove confusion and get a baseline.

Action: Beef, eggs, bacon, chicken, fish, salt, water.

CCC Check-In: Energy, hunger, cravings, digestion, sleep.

Day 2 — Hydration + Salt

Focus: Stop fake hunger and smooth energy dips.

Action: Drink water early, salt food well, avoid grazing.

Day 3 — Craving Control

Focus: Beat the urge window.

Action: Wait 10 minutes, drink water, eat protein if hungry, walk 5 minutes.

Days 4–7: Find The Pattern

Day 4 — Sleep Reset

No late snacks. Dim lights earlier. Phone down earlier. Keep dinner simple.

Day 5 — Movement Day

Walk 20 minutes, stretch, do mobility, or complete a light workout.

Day 6 — Pattern Check

Review your best energy day, worst craving day, best sleep, digestion, and mood.

Day 7 — Lock The System

Pick your repeatable meal timing, food list, workout days, sleep target, and check-in time.

Use CCC During The Reset

Don’t just do the reset — track it. Use Carnivore Command Center to log energy, cravings, sleep, digestion, mood, soreness, and readiness.
