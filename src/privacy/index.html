Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Carnivore Command Center collects, uses, and protects information provided by users of the platform.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including account details such as your email address and information you choose to enter into the platform, such as health-related tracking inputs, notes, and usage data associated with your account.

How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the platform
  • To generate dashboard insights, scores, summaries, and recommendations
  • To communicate with users about product updates or account-related matters
  • To protect the security and integrity of the platform

Health-related information

Carnivore Command Center may allow users to enter wellness or health-related tracking information. This information is used to provide product functionality and user-requested insights. CCC is not a medical provider and does not provide medical advice.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the platform, such as hosting, infrastructure, analytics, or authentication providers, subject to appropriate safeguards and only as reasonably necessary to provide the service.

Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security, and users should understand that internet-based services carry inherent risk.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your choices

Users may request account-related assistance or deletion inquiries through the applicable support or business contact channel associated with the platform.

Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
