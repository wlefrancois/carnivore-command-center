The Metabolic Readiness Framework

Carnivore Command Center tracks the signals that influence metabolic readiness, recovery, and daily performance.

Why metabolic readiness matters

Energy, inflammation, digestion, and recovery are influenced by a combination of sleep, hydration, nutrition, and stress signals. Tracking these signals consistently reveals patterns that are otherwise difficult to see.

1

Sleep

Sleep duration and sleep quality strongly influence hormonal balance, inflammation, and recovery.

2

Hydration

Hydration affects circulation, electrolyte balance, and cellular performance.

3

Inflammation

Inflammation signals can indicate digestive issues, overtraining, or poor recovery.

The readiness scoring model

Carnivore Command Center converts daily signals into a readiness score that helps identify limiting factors.

Signal Tracking

Users log daily signals including sleep, hydration, inflammation, digestion, and energy.

Pattern Detection

CCC analyzes patterns across multiple days to identify signals associated with higher readiness.

Actionable Insights

Insights highlight what signals may be limiting tomorrow's readiness score.

Signals currently tracked

Sleep

Hours slept and perceived sleep quality.

Hydration

Total fluid intake and hydration habits.

Inflammation

Perceived inflammation, soreness, or digestive signals.

Energy

Daily energy levels and fatigue patterns.

Digestion

Digestive comfort and signals related to diet.

Mood

Mental clarity and emotional state.
