See CCC In Action

Carnivore Command Center helps users understand readiness, track daily signals, and choose the right mode for the day.

What the product does
  • Tracks daily signals in under a minute
  • Calculates a readiness score
  • Surfaces limiting factors like sleep or hydration
  • Guides users into Focus, Recovery, Workout, Travel, or Damage Control mode
